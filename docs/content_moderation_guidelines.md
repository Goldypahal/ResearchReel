# Content Moderation Guidelines

Last Updated: July 1, 2026

To maintain a high-quality, professional, and respectful environment for scientific discussion, ResearchReel enforces strict content moderation guidelines. All users, creators, and moderators must adhere to these policies.

## 1. Acceptable Content
* **Academic Relevance**: All posts, document uploads, and reels must be related to scientific, academic, or professional research.
* **Respectful Discourse**: Critical feedback must be constructive and respectful. Personal attacks, harassment, and academic snobbery are strictly prohibited.
* **Accuracy and Integrity**: Present research truthfully. Misrepresenting experimental findings or fabricating citation sources is ground for immediate suspension.

## 2. Prohibited Content
Any post, reel, comment, or message containing the following will be removed, and the author may face account restrictions:
* **Academic Dishonesty**: Plagiarism, paper-mill publications, or falsified research results.
* **Harassment and Bullying**: Hate speech, derogatory language, or targeted abuse of researchers.
* **Inappropriate Media**: Non-academic video clips, advertising/spam, violence, or sexually explicit content.
* **Security Threats**: Executables, scripts, or files containing malware (automatically filtered by our virus scanner).

## 3. Reporting and Flagging System
Users can flag posts or comments for violating these guidelines:
1. **User Action**: Clicking the "Report" button on a post, selecting a reason, and providing details.
2. **System Action**: Flagged posts are routed to the Moderation Dashboard and logged in the audit trail.
3. **Moderator Actions**: A moderator reviews the report. They can:
   * **Dismiss**: Clear the report if no violation is found.
   * **Remove Post**: Delete the offending content.
   * **Suspend/Restrict User**: Temporarily or permanently disable user account capabilities or verification.

## 4. Appeals Process
If your content was removed or your account restricted, you can submit an appeal request to `moderation@researchreel.com` containing the post ID and justification.
