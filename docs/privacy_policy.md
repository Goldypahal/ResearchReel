# Privacy Policy (DPDP Act 2023 Compliant)

Last Updated: July 1, 2026

ResearchReel ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and protect your information in compliance with the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act, 2023 of India, and other applicable global regulations.

## 1. Consent and Data Principal Rights
As a Data Principal, by using our platform, you give free, specific, informed, unconditional, and unambiguous consent to the processing of your personal data as outlined in this policy.
You have the right to:
* **Access**: Request information about the personal data processed.
* **Correction & Erasure**: Correct, complete, or request erasure of your personal data.
* **Withdrawal of Consent**: Withdraw your consent at any time.

## 2. Personal Data We Collect
We collect the following personal data:
* **Account Information**: Email, username, password hash, full name, and bio.
* **Academic Credentials**: ORCID iD, institution name, and department.
* **User-Generated Content**: Uploaded research papers (PDF/DOCX), summary requests, reels (MP4), comments, and reactions.
* **Metadata & Log Data**: IP address, device type, browser, page views, and access times.

## 3. Purpose of Processing
We process personal data to:
* Provide and maintain ResearchReel services.
* Verify researcher credentials (ORCID / academic status).
* Generate AI summaries and index documents for semantic search.
* Prevent fraud, malware uploads, and system abuse.

## 4. Data Sharing and Transfer
We do not sell your personal data. We only share personal data with trusted third parties for service delivery (e.g., managed database hosting, cloud storage, Sentry error tracking, PostHog analytics) under strict data protection agreements.

## 5. Security Measures
We implement administrative, technical, and physical security measures, including:
* HTTPS/TLS edge encryption for all traffic.
* Password hashing via Argon2.
* Malware and virus scanning on file uploads.
* Regular security scans and vulnerability assessments.

## 6. Retention and Deletion
We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes of processing or as required by law. Upon withdrawal of consent or deletion of your account, your data will be permanently and securely deleted from our databases.

If you have any questions, feedback, or grievances regarding your data privacy, please contact our designated Grievance Officer at:
