# ResearchReel FAQ & Help Center

Welcome to the ResearchReel FAQ! Here you will find answers to the most common questions about sharing, discovering, and discussing academic research on our platform.

---

## 🚀 Getting Started

### What is ResearchReel?
ResearchReel is a microservice-based, cloud-native platform designed for scientists, professors, and students to share research via short-form videos (Reels) and interactive research documents.

### How do I register?
1. Click the **Register** button on the homepage.
2. Enter your email, username, and password.
3. Verify your email using the OTP sent to your mailbox.

### How can I verify my account?
We support institutional verification and ORCID linking.
* **ORCID**: Go to your profile settings, click **Link ORCID**, and authenticate. This grants you a **Scholar** badge.
* **Institution email**: Register or verify using a valid `.edu` or institutional email address.

## 📹 Sharing Reels & Papers

### What is the maximum video length?
Reels are optimized for high-impact, bite-sized science communications. Videos must be between **30 and 60 seconds** long, and up to **200 MB** in size. Supported formats include `.mp4` and `.mov`.

### How do I link a research paper to my Reel?
When uploading a Reel in the **Create** tab, you can drag and drop a PDF/DOCX copy of your paper or enter a valid Crossref DOI. Our system automatically:
1. Uploads the document to our secure storage.
2. Ingests it into our AI/RAG service.
3. Generates an instant summary and key points visible to everyone watching your Reel.

## 🤖 AI Summary & Q&A

### How does the AI Summary work?
Once a research paper is attached to a post, our Gemini/Cohere-powered AI model extracts:
* A structured 3-sentence summary of the paper.
* 3-5 core key takeaways.
This is displayed right alongside your Reel.

### Can I ask questions about a paper?
Yes! Click the **Ask AI** button underneath any paper card to open an interactive Q&A chat. You can query the paper's contents directly, and our RAG pipeline will provide grounded answers with context from the PDF.

## 🛡️ Moderation & Safety

### How does ResearchReel stay secure?
* **Malware Scanning**: All file uploads are automatically scanned for viruses and malicious payloads before storage.
* **Content Filters**: Text and comments are checked in real-time against keyword and sentiment guidelines to prevent harassment or spam.
* **Report System**: Users can click "Report" on any post. Flagged content is sent to our moderator queue for rapid review.
